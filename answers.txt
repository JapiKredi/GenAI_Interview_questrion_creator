It helps prevent waterborne diseases, improves hygiene, and promotes overall well-being. However, many people around the world still lack access to safe drinking water and proper sanitation facilities. Water scarcity affects over 40% of the global population, and climate change is projected to exacerbate this issue. Without proper action, by 2050, one in four people may face recurring water shortages. Additionally, poor sanitation practices and pollution contribute to water contamination, making it unsafe for consumption.
